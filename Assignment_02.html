Signals

When visitors arrive to a site , they want to know if they are at the right place. 37 Signals helps people determine that immediately by noting who can benefit from their solutions (designers, consultants, manufacturers, etc.). And they nicely their offerings and descriptive teasers for each tool, setting expectations what to expect beyond each link.

In addition to screenshots 37 Signals provides third-party endorsements of its awesomeness through a seal that states, “Trusted by millions of people in over 30 countries,” along with a series of succinct, relevant testimonials. Providing social evidence is an effective way to gain credibility and trust on the Web because people naturally take comfort in going with the masses.

Lesson learned

Words brand your business, better or worse. Take a page from 37 Signals; their content is kept simple, practical and friendly, which is consistent with their apps. They are not just stating they provide frustration-free experiences, they are demonstrating it.
